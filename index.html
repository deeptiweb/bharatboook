BOOK LIFE

Bhartayan

autobiograhy,  Feb 28, 2015
WorldSmith Publication
page-count:558, ISBN no:938479102-4

Short description:
This is my autobiography.I am a simple poor person born in small town barber's house,living in Pandharur.I have no identity, i haven't recieved any awards, i haven't studied much. But a lot of troubles came in my life which has been described in this book. One more thing, all these incidents are true. There is no pretence, and i didn't hide anything. ... I am as i have told. My mother has suffered more than me, that's why this book narrates the story of my mother from her birth til her death. Under which everthing about my life comes to the fore.Now i am living a happy life.This is result of my karma, by reading this book you will see happiness even in sarrow, that is my claim..
